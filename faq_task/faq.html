Frequently asked
questions?

Everything you need to know before enrolling. From eligibility and course details to internships and certifications, we've got you covered.

Are the live classes recorded?

Yes. All live sessions are recorded and made available on the BAOIAM Learning Management System (LMS). If you miss a class or wish to revise any topic, you can access the recordings anytime during your program.

Can working professionals attend the program?

Definitely. Our programs are designed to accommodate both students and working professionals. With weekend live classes, recorded sessions, and flexible learning access, you can learn at your own pace without affecting your work schedule.

Is EMI or installment payment available?

Yes. We offer flexible payment options, including EMI plans for selected programs, making quality education affordable and accessible. Our admissions team will guide you through the available payment options based on the program you choose.

Will I receive a certificate after completing the program?

Yes. Upon successful completion of the program and meeting the required criteria, you'll receive a course completion certificate from BAOIAM, which can strengthen your resume, LinkedIn profile, and professional portfolio.

Do I need any prior experience?

No prior experience is required for most of our beginner-friendly programs. Whether you're a student, recent graduate, or someone planning a career transition, our structured curriculum helps you learn from scratch.
