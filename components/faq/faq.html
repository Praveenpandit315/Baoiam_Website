Frequently asked
questions?

Everything you need to know before enrolling. From eligibility and course details to internships and certifications, we've got you covered.

Who can apply for internships or courses?

Students, graduates, and working professionals are welcome to apply. We have opportunities for all skill levels.

Are the courses industry-recognized?

Our courses are designed in collaboration with industry experts and are recognized by leading organizations.

Will I get guidance and mentorship during the internship?

Yes. Every intern is paired with experienced mentors who provide regular guidance, feedback, and support throughout the program. Our mentorship model ensures you not only complete assigned tasks but also gain valuable insights into industry practices, career development, and skill enhancement. You'll have access to one-on-one sessions, group discussions, and progress reviews to help you grow professionally.

Can I apply for multiple domains at the same time?

Yes, you are welcome to apply for internships in more than one domain. We encourage applicants to explore different areas of interest. However, final selection will depend on your skills, availability, and how well you meet the requirements of each domain.

Are the internships paid or unpaid?

Internship compensation varies depending on the position and domain. Some roles are paid, while others are unpaid but come with valuable training, mentorship, and certification. The payment details for each internship are clearly mentioned in the respective opening, so applicants can review them before applying.
